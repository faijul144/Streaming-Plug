F.A.Q

Answers to some quetions that you might have
  • What Is Streaming Plug?

    Streaming Plug is a new social media platform designed specifically for video streaming, live streaming, vlogging, and broadcasting. With just a small, simple, and miniscule monthly subscription fee, our content creators are able to earn and keep 100% of their earnings and tips from the content that they stream through the platform.

  • Who is Basic for?

    Basic is for streamers who want to allow their fans to support them at flexible monthly amounts, and aren’t planning to provide a lot of different benefits at different membership tiers.

  • Who is Advance for?

    Advance is for streamers who want to build a robust, thriving membership business with different benefits at different membership tiers, and who want more tools to help them grow their business.

  • Who is Premium for?

    Premium is for established creators and creative businesses looking for a higher level of service and advanced features. The Premium plan has a limited number of openings at this time. We’ll be rolling it out gradually as spots become available.

  • How easy is it to move back and forth between the different plans?

    You can switch between Basic and Advance at any time, after disabling any features that aren't included in Lite, such as multiple membership tiers, and Special Offers if you have an active offer running. For creators who join Premium, there is a minimum three month commitment before you can switch to a different plan.

  • What payment methods are available for patrons to pledge to me?

    PayPal,Visa, or credit cards.

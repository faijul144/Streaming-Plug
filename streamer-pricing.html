F.A.Q

Answers to some quetions that you might have
  • What Is Streaming Plug?

    Streaming Plug is a new social media platform designed specifically for video streaming, live streaming, vlogging, and broadcasting. With just a small, simple, and miniscule monthly subscription fee, our content creators are able to earn and keep 100% of their earnings and tips from the content that they stream through the platform.

  • Who is Free for?

    Free is for streamers who want to allow their fans to support them even if they can't choose one of the paid different membership tiers at this moment.

  • Who is Silver for?

    Silver is for streamers who want to allow their fans to support them at flexible monthly amounts, and aren’t planning to provide a lot of different benefits at different membership tiers.

  • Who is Gold for?

    Gold is for streamers who want to build a robust, thriving membership business with different benefits at different membership tiers, and who want more tools to help them grow their business.

  • Who is Platinum for?

    Platinum is for established creators and creative businesses looking for a higher level of service and advanced features.

  • Who is VIP for?

    VIP is for Clebrities, NGO's and Enterprises who are in need of more or specific features.

  • How easy is it to move back and forth between the different plans?

    You can switch between different packages at any time, after disabling any features that aren't included in package, such as multiple membership tiers, and Special Offers if you have an active offer running.

  • What payment methods are available for streamers to pledge to me?

    PayPal or any international famous credit card service powered by Stripe.

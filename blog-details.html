3 Reasons Why Digital Streaming is Exploding in Popularity in 2021

As coronavirus barreled down on the world at the start of 2020, people wondered how they were ever going to connect, share information, and feel entertained, from their homes and apartments as they worked to socially distance.

With online streaming already on the rise, as Nielsen’s U.S. reported in 2018 that 42% of the population in the U.S. now has live-streamed online content, the pandemic merely exacerbated the inevitable: the future resides in the ability to consume real-time content through our phones, TVs, and laptops.

But, in order to consume this exhilarating streamed content that we sign onto social media every day to see, there needs to be the actual content creators that sit down and curate what we love and desire. That’s why more people than ever are showing an interest in using streaming as another funnel of income, especially during a time when income reliability is crumbling.

Here are 3 major reasons why digital streaming is exploding in popularity this year (and will continue to do so for years to come):

  • 1. All You Need is an Electronic Device and Internet: Streaming is another viable funnel that requires nothing but the mobile device and internet you are already paying for. You don’t need to pay thousands of dollars to obtain products or do anything else to get this kind of income flowing. With no startup costs, anyone can do it.
  • 2. More People Than Ever Are Consuming Content Online: While we sit at home and do our parts to slow the spread, more people than ever are relying on the internet to make them feel connected and entertained. The in-person entertainment we took for granted has been undercut to keep us all safe, which is why more people than ever are online, hoping they will find the content they seek. It’s nice to have an escape from reality, if only for a moment.
  • 3. Streaming is Becoming Easier for Creators to Do: The tools and variety of platforms at your disposal are making it easier than ever before to be a video streamer. The only problem is that many of these sites don’t exactly have your long-term success in mind. They are set up to turn a profit, and they are surely profiting big-league today.

Here are Streaming Plug, we do not support the profiting that occurs when platforms hijack streamers and their intimate connections with their audience. Instead, we believe that this following is something that unequivocally belongs to you and the hard work you have put into your internet presence. That’s why we are launching the first-ever streaming social media site where creators get to keep 100% of the commission.

Every streamer gets 100% of the tips with no deductions, and no hidden fees. It’s you and your content, the way it was always meant to be. With a massive, growing user-base that is only going to get bigger thanks to the pandemic, we encourage you to consider this side hustle as a real reality for yourself in 2021.
